# System monitor & logs

A quick way to check what your system is doing, right from the bar. Click
CPU, RAM, or the temperature reading and it opens straight to that view.
No digging through menus for the right screen.

## Getting there

Click any of the CPU, RAM, or temperature numbers on the bar to jump
straight to that view. Or open System Monitor from the main menu for
CPU, RAM, or Sensors specifically, or System Logs to browse what's been
happening on your machine.

## The views

Every screen shares a strip across the top showing all four views, so you
always know what else is available and how to get to it. Press `Tab` to
move to the next one.

- **CPU**: usage for every core, plus the overall total, updating live.
- **RAM**: how much memory is used, available, cached, and how your swap
  looks.
- **Sensors**: every temperature reading your machine reports, disk
  included.
- **Logs**: pick something running on your system from a list, and watch
  its activity scroll by live. Scroll back through what's already
  happened, or jump straight to the very latest.

Press `[` or `]` to make it update faster or slower. Press `q` or `Esc`
to quit, or to step back out of the log view one level.
