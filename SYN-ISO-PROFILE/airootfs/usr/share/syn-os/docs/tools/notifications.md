# Notifications

The little popups in the top-right corner, "Screenshot saved," "Wi-Fi
connected," and so on, come from the same place across the whole system.
They're themed to match whatever look you've got active, and they show up
the moment the desktop starts, before you've even opened anything.

Regular notifications disappear after a few seconds. Important ones, like
a failed connection or an install error, stay on screen until you dismiss
them yourself, so you don't miss something that actually needs your
attention.
