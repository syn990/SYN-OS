# Display & screens

Manage your monitors from Preferences → Display & Screens. It's a live
menu, one entry per monitor actually connected to your machine right
now.

For each monitor you can turn it on or off, set it as your primary
display, choose its resolution and refresh rate, rotate it, and position
it relative to your other monitors, left of, right of, above, or below.

Turning a monitor off is remembered. Next time you log in, it stays off,
unless it was your only monitor, in which case SYN-OS won't let you turn
off your last remaining screen.
