# Services

Turn any background service on your system on or off, from a real,
live list of what's actually installed, not a fixed shortlist of the
usual suspects.

Find it under Preferences → Services. Pick a service and you'll see
whether it's currently enabled and running. You're only ever offered the
one action that makes sense, enable a disabled service or disable an
enabled one, and it asks you to confirm before making the change.

You'll get a notification once it's done, or if something went wrong.
